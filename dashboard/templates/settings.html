BUDGETS

Two independent budgets — shadow is your sandbox, real is what the wallet can spend. Lane splits are configured in config/config.yaml (scalping 60 / event 30 / longshot 10).

RESET SHADOW HISTORY

Wipes shadow positions only (open & closed) so a new shadow test run starts at zero.
Real positions are NOT touched — rows with is_real=1 stay exactly as they are, so live trading is unaffected. Signals, price ticks, ollama stats, cross-references, and system logs are also kept because real-mode risk checks depend on them (stale-price detection, concentration limits, validator drift).
The allocator is resynced: shadow lanes' deployed drops to 0, real lanes are recomputed from their still-open rows. This cannot be undone.
